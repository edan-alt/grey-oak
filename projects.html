Buziga Condominium Project - Golden View Villas

Welcome to Buziga Condominium Project – Golden View Villas — where luxury meets breathtaking views.

These beautifully designed contemporary homes feature elegant earth-toned finishes, crafted by SASA Architects and detailed by Grey Oak Limited. Each of the four exclusive 3-level villas, complete with a penthouse, sits on a spacious 720 sqm plot, offering stunning views from the terrace down to the garden.

Inside, enjoy a seamless blend of comfort and style—from a master walk-in closet that flows into a private bathroom, to a sleek staircase finished with granite, glass, and stainless steel. The expansive rooftop deck provides the perfect space for hosting and relaxing.

Golden View Villas offer refined living in a truly elevated setting.
