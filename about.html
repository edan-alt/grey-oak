Our Story

Headquartered on Plot 23, Ntinda Road in Kampala, Grey Oak Limited is a full-service construction and engineering firm. We combine modern engineering practice with rigorous quality control to deliver homes, commercial spaces, and specialised structures that stand the test of time.

Founded in 2014, we have grown from a small team of dedicated professionals to one of Uganda's most trusted construction companies. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and client satisfaction has earned us a reputation for excellence across residential, commercial, and specialised projects.

Whether you're building your dream home, developing a commercial property, or undertaking a specialised construction project, Grey Oak Limited brings the expertise, integrity, and dedication to make your vision a reality.
